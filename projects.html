project #1 17/2/25

audrey's world

I made this website as my application for DevSoc training program. Theme was "Around the World", so naturally the first thing that popped into my head was a globe.

I learned to use a 3D modelling platform called Spline. It was just like learning how to use Photoshop... there were so many functions and dealing with 3D planes on a computer screen was very hard. However, it was actually really fun and made me feel accomplished when I finished designing my globe.

As you hover over the countries I've visited, more information will pop up!
